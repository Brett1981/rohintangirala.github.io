About

I am a web developer, app developer, and all-around coder.

Hey there! I am Rohin Tangirala, a sophomore at Saint Francis High School, with a passion for hacking and coding. Whether I am at a hackathon or working on a project on my own time, I love solving problems and making products that help people. Music and photography are also passions of mine.

Portfolio

I am always working on new projects that demonstrate my passion for coding. Here is a selection of a few that I have worked on:

Day Tripper

Created with a friend at BASEHacks 2016, Day Tripper is a chatbot that simplifies planning for a short day trip, providing transit time and weather. The project won a couple of awards at BASEHacks.

HeadsUp

A simple, conversational web app that provides transit time and weather at a destination for a short trip. Inspired by Day Tripper.

  Github   Try it out

Finals Calculator

Student tool for calculating what he or she needs on a final exam. I created this when I needed a convenient way to plan for finals at school, and I hope it will help numerous other students as well.
